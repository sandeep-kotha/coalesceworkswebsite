Project Volt — Energy Sovereignty

Britain imports over 40% of its gas. When global markets spike, British households and industry bear the cost. Project Volt changes the equation.

Our transformer-based load models ingest real-time data from the National Grid, weather systems, and industrial demand signals. We forecast consumption with sub-hourly precision, enabling operators to balance renewable generation—wind, solar, nuclear—without gas peakers. The result: a grid that can run on 90% domestic generation, resilient to geopolitical shocks.

Project Ceres — Agricultural Sovereignty

The UK produces less than 60% of its food. Brexit exposed supply chain fragility; climate change will intensify it. Project Ceres reimagines British agriculture.

Computer vision systems mounted on tractors and drones detect crop stress, disease, and nutrient deficiency at scale. Robotics handle planting, weeding, and harvesting with precision that cuts water use by 95% and eliminates chemical runoff. Vertical farms in disused industrial sites produce vegetables year-round. The goal: food sovereignty by 2035.

Project Aethelgard — Health Sovereignty

The NHS treats illness; it rarely prevents it. Britain spends £150bn annually on healthcare while life expectancy stalls. Project Aethelgard shifts the paradigm.

Multimodal agentic AI synthesises patient records, imaging, genomics, and real-time vitals into a unified care model. It predicts deterioration days before crisis, flags drug interactions, and recommends personalised interventions. Clinicians remain in control—the system augments, never replaces.

Project Bullion — Financial Sovereignty

British investors pay Capital Gains Tax on gold ETFs and foreign coins. Physical British Sovereigns are CGT-exempt. Project Bullion makes it accessible.

Our distributed ledger records custody of physical Sovereigns in UK vaults. Each token represents fractional ownership with full legal title. HMRC recognises the structure. Investors can hold £1 or £1m in tax-efficient form, with instant settlement and transparent audit.
